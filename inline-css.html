What is listening?

Listening is the ability to accurately receive and interpret message in the communication process. Listening is a key to all effective communication. • Listening is the ability to understand and respond effectively to oral communication. • Listening is the process of receiving, constructing meaning from, and responding to spoken and/or non-verbal messages. • It is a form of communication an active process. • Listening skill is absorption of the meanings of words and sentences by the brain. • It is leads to the understanding of facts and ideas.
